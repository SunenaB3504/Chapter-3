🔢 Multiplication Facts 🔢

Multiplication facts are like small multiplication problems that are good to remember. They help us solve bigger problems faster!

Let's look at some examples from the book:

  • 6 x 2 = 12
  • 2 x 4 = 8

We will practice these with games and tables later!

Let's practice these with a game!

⏱️ Times Table Challenge! ⏱️

How to Play:

  1. Look at the multiplication question (like 3 x 4 = ?).
  2. Type your answer in the box.
  3. Click the "Check Answer" button (or press Enter).
  4. If you're right on the first try, you get 5 points! ✨
  5. Click "New Question" to try another one!

🧠 Memory Match! 🧠

How to Play:

  1. Click on two cards to flip them over.
  2. One card has a question (like 5 x 6), the other has the answer (like 30).
  3. If the question and answer match, the cards stay flipped and you get 10 points! 🎉
  4. If they don't match, they flip back over. Try to remember where they are!
  5. Match all the pairs to win!
