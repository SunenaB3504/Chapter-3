🤔 What is Multiplication? 🤔

Multiplication is like a super-fast way of doing addition!

Imagine you have 3 groups of apples, and each group has 4 apples.

🍎🍎🍎🍎 + 🍎🍎🍎🍎 + 🍎🍎🍎🍎

You could count them all one by one, or you could add them: 4 + 4 + 4 = 12.

Multiplication lets us write this even faster:

3 groups × 4 apples per group = 12 apples

So, 3 × 4 = 12.

Multiplication helps us when we have equal groups of things. The '×' symbol means "times" or "groups of".

How to Play:

  5. Click "Check Answer" to see if you got it right!
